<Accordion client:load title="Who can join the workshops?">
Our sessions are open to people of all ages and abilities.  
No previous experience is needed — just a willingness to have a go.
</Accordion>

<Accordion client:load title="Do I need to bring anything?">
We provide all the tools and materials.  
Just wear comfortable clothes that you don’t mind getting a bit messy.
</Accordion>

<Accordion client:load title="Where do the sessions take place?">
All our workshops are held at our fully equipped workspace at Units 10 and 11 Port Talbot Workshops.  
We'll include directions and accessibility info soon.  
[View Full Address and Map](/en/contact#map)
</Accordion>

<Accordion client:load title="How much does it cost?">
Most of our workshops are free, thanks to funding and donations.  
Some specialised sessions may have a small materials fee — we’ll always let you know in advance.
</Accordion>

<Accordion client:load title="What if I’ve never done anything like this before?">
That’s exactly who we’re here for!  
You’ll be supported by friendly tutors and other learners.
</Accordion>
